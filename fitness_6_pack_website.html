Welcome to Your Fitness Journey

Getting fit and achieving 6-pack abs requires a combination of proper workouts, healthy eating, and discipline. Here, we provide a detailed guide to help you transform your body and improve overall health.

Follow these step-by-step routines and tips consistently to see visible results in a few weeks.

Workout Routines

Crunches

Crunches target your upper abs. Lie on your back with knees bent and lift your shoulders off the floor using your abs.

Plank

Plank strengthens your core. Hold your body straight with elbows and toes on the ground for 30-60 seconds.

Leg Raises

Leg raises target lower abs. Lie on your back and lift legs straight up, then slowly lower them down without touching the floor.

Bicycle Crunches

Bicycle crunches work upper, lower, and oblique abs. Move your legs in a cycling motion while touching opposite elbow to knee.

Diet Plan

Protein Rich Foods

Include eggs, chicken, fish, and lentils. Proteins help build muscle and repair tissues.

Complex Carbs

Consume oats, brown rice, and whole grains. They provide energy for intense workouts.

Healthy Fats

Include nuts, seeds, olive oil, and avocado. Healthy fats are essential for hormones and energy.

Fruits & Vegetables

Eat a variety of colorful vegetables and fruits for vitamins, minerals, and fiber.

Fitness Tips

  • Stay consistent with your workouts.
  • Drink plenty of water daily.
  • Get 7-8 hours of sleep each night.
  • Avoid sugary drinks and junk food.
  • Track your progress with weekly photos.
